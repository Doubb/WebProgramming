"There's no sign of his success slowing down"

-SBS PopAsia HQ

If you're a fan of Japanese pop, Kenshi Yonezu's name should be pretty familiar to you by now. He's been topping the charts for a couple years now, and there's no sign of his success slowing down.

The song was written for drama series Unnatural but it's taken on a life of its own, with over 153 million YouTube views, and over 1.5 million downloads in Japan alone.

"Currently No.1 among J-pop artists by a long shot"

-Billboard Japan

Kenshi Yonezu is doing well, with his most popular video ‘Lemon’ having been watched more than 435m times on YouTube since its release in February 2018.

As a guide to how his more recent videos have performed, ‘Spirits of the Sea’ has done 43m views since its release in late May 2019, while ‘Paprika’ has done 7.8m views this weekend after its release on Friday 9 August.
